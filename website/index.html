archivejump

browser extension that redirects article links to archived versions when you hit paywalls.

install

firefox chrome (manual)

how it works

automatically detects article links from major news sites. when you click a link and hit a paywall, archivejump offers to redirect you to an archived version from archive.ph or wayback machine.

supported sites

new york times, wall street journal, washington post, the atlantic, the guardian, reuters, bloomberg, financial times, and others.

privacy

no tracking, no data collection. your preferences stay on your device.
